Welcome to "Invasion 83: Alien Assault!" – where you become the ultimate alien invader in a thrilling retro adventure! Inspired by the Vic20 classic 'Blitz,' it's your mission to blast cities and avoid a fiery crash landing.

Created by Kiree Bellamy, Kim Hanlon, Angela Siniavskaja, Ahmad Al Hindi, Amir Ahmed, and Catherine Parrish.

Take control as the alien pilot and clear the city below with your bombs. Remember, only one bomb at a time – no spam allowed! As you descend, the pressure's on: clear all buildings or face defeat. Succeed and soar to the next level!

Controls: Use number keys to choose your difficulty, Space or click to drop bombs, Escape to pause.

Press Any Key to Begin the Invasion!
